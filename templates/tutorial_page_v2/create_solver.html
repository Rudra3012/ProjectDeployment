STEP:1

You can see the Crossword Puzzle on Screen with Details Author,Description,User rating etc.Click on "Solve" button to solve that crossword puzzle.

STEP:2

Now to start solve crossword puzzle click on Start Button.After clicking on start Timer will start .You can see the Attempt for This crossword puzzle.

STEP:3

You have to fill words as per Horizontal clues and Vertical Clues.

STEP:4

If you entered wrong it will show the message that your answer is worng.Now you can enter word again.After Entered all correct words press on the Submit Button.

STEP:5

After Solving Crossword you can rate the puzzle on basis 1 to 5 .After click on Submit Rating and Press on "Home" button to redirect to Homepage.
